About Quatro

A Cocktail is Born.

Late nights. Unforgettable moments. The perfect sip. We loved to drink, but we craved something different — something refreshing, exciting, and far from the ordinary. So we made our own rules, mixed our own drinks, and created a cocktail designed for those who want more than just another drink.

Cheers!

Every Quatro can is crafted with care, using high-quality ingredients and just the right balance of sweetness and smoothness. Whether you’re lounging by the pool, dancing with friends, or simply unwinding after a long day, there’s a Quatro for every vibe.

Our lineup features five signature flavours, each inspired by the spirit of celebration and adventure. From the tropical burst of Piña Colada, to the fruity sparkle of Peach Bellini, to the bold kick of Cherry Bomb, every sip invites you to live life to the fullest.

At Quatro, we believe that great moments deserve great drinks. No matter where you are or who you're with, we’re here to add a splash of fun, flavour, and freedom to your day. So grab a Quatro, raise your can, and celebrate without limits.

And guess what!?

Quatro is more than just a cocktail — it's a lifestyle. Inspired by iconic brands that transcended their products, we're building an experience that's all about flavour, culture, and unforgettable moments.

That's why we've expanded beyond drinks. Now, you can bring the essence of Quatro into your life with aromatic candles, branded caps, and pop sockets — because Quatro isn't just something you sip, it's something you live.
